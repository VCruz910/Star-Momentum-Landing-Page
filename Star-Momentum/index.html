STAR MOMENTUM

Star momentum was born out of a desire for an easily accessible fun experience that can be played in just a moment. A simple game with hidden complexity that allows you to easily engross yourself in some harmless escapism, while evoking memories of the golden era of arcade games for those that were fortunate enough to witness it firsthand, and introducing the pure and minimalistic gameplay concept of that bygone time to the younger generations that so often find themselves overloaded with information.
The mechanics of the game were meant to be simple; every time you move your energy depletes. Pick up energy while dodging asteroids to get as far as possible and set a high score! Use W and S or the up and down arrows to move.
